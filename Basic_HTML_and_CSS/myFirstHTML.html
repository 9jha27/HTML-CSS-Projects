Laura's Coding Adventures

In the Beginning...

I really couldn't figure out what I wanted to do. I wanted to learn so many things to take advantage of the time I had off from work from the symphony.
I had always wanted to learn to code, and I was immediately hooked. I think this is the case with many people, but I had many fellow musicians who had switched to coding/programming careers, so I thought I would give it a try!

Inspirations

I have deep roots in the Korean culture, even though I have only been there a handful of times. My father always told me stories about simple times and about sharing a small bedroom with 6 brothers. I will never forget the feelings I have when he tells me stories about growing up, playing without technology. I am inspired by simplistic times: straw rooves, white snow, tall grass. It is my goal to design your website with the utmost care and the most simplistic touch.
